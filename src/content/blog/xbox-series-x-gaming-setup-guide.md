# Xbox Series X Gaming Setup Guide

Complete guide to setting up your Xbox Series X for the best gaming experience.

## About Xbox Series X

The Xbox Series X is Microsoft's most powerful gaming console ever, delivering 4K gaming at up to 120fps.

## What's in the Box

- Xbox Series X console
- Xbox Wireless Controller
- Power cable
- HDMI 2.1 cable
- Quick Start Guide

## Initial Setup

Getting your Xbox Series X ready takes just a few minutes. Connect it to power and your TV, then follow the on-screen setup wizard.

## Network Connection

Connect to your internet via Ethernet or Wi-Fi. Ethernet is recommended for the best performance.

## Game Pass Benefits

With Xbox Game Pass, you get access to hundreds of games including day-one releases.

## Performance Tips

- Update your console regularly
- Use SSD storage for faster loading
- Adjust graphics settings in games
- Keep your gaming area well-ventilated
