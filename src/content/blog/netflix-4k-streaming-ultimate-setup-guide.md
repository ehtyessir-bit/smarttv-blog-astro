# Netflix 4K Streaming - Ultimate Setup Guide

Learn how to maximize your Netflix 4K streaming experience on your Smart TV with this complete guide.

## What is 4K Streaming?

4K Ultra HD streaming delivers video at 3840×2160 resolution, providing four times the detail of standard HD.

## Netflix 4K Packages

Netflix offers different subscription tiers. The Premium plan includes 4K content for up to 4 simultaneous streams.

## Requirements for 4K

- Netflix Premium subscription
- 4K compatible Smart TV
- High-speed internet (25 Mbps minimum)
- Compatible streaming device

## Best 4K Content on Netflix

Netflix has thousands of 4K titles including documentaries, movies, and series.

## Setup Steps

1. Subscribe to Netflix Premium
2. Update your TV firmware
3. Download Netflix app
4. Sign in with your account
5. Start watching 4K content
